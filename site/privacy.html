Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Nicolai Tufar built the IP Calculator app as an open-source application. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Information Collection and Use

The IP Calculator app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All computation happens locally on your device. The app requires no internet permission and never communicates with any server.

Network Access

The app does not request any network permissions. It functions entirely offline with no internet access required.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.

Data Storage

The app does not write any data to device storage. No files, preferences, or cache are created or accessed beyond what the Android system manages automatically.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not address anyone under the age of 13. It does not collect personally identifiable information from anyone, including children.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact
